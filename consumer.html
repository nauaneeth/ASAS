Frequently Asked Questions

  • What’s the Amazon Prime offer?

    If you’re a new or existing du Home customer, with a subscription to one of the below, you’ll enjoy Amazon Prime for 12 months on us. du Home: Basic, Advanced and Ultimate. du Home Light: Basic, Advanced and Ultimate.

  • How can I subscribe to this offer??

    If you have a Home subscription:

    Step 1 - Log into Myaccount.du.ae with your du Home account number. In case you have multiple du accounts, please make sure you log in with your du Home account.
    Step 2 - Click on the ‘Amazon Prime’ banner, which will direct you to the Amazon Prime page to complete your registration.
    Step 3 - After signing up, download the Amazon Prime app from the App Store or Google Play Store to start enjoying Prime.

  • How long is the offer valid for??

    The discount will apply starting from your second bill for a period of 12 months.

  • Can existing customers subscribe to the new du home plans?

    Yes, customers can migrate from their current home plans by logging in to My Account, pin the location they live in, select the package and submit the request online..

  • I'm with another service provider, can I change to du?

    Simply choose one of our flexible du Home Light plans and we'll manage everything else, including moving your services over from your current provider.

  • Can I cancel my plan after I subscribe to it??

    Yes, you can call 155 or visit our stores to cancel your plan at any time. However, if you're on a contract, an early termination fee will apply.
